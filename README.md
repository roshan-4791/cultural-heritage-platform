Cultural Heritage Preservation Platform
Overview

This project focuses on preserving the cultural heritage of local communities.
Students collect stories, folk tales, recipes, songs, traditions, and life experiences from village elders through recorded interviews.
These recorded videos are organized, categorized, and showcased on a YouTube channel with subtitles for better accessibility.
The website acts as a digital archive where anyone can explore the collected stories easily.

Objectives

Preserve local heritage by documenting oral traditions.

Encourage student participation in community-based storytelling.

Publish interviews on YouTube with subtitles in multiple languages.

Provide a simple and accessible digital archive for all stories.

Strengthen connections between younger and older generations.

Features

Upload and organize recorded interview videos

Categorized story listing (folk tales, recipes, songs, life stories)

Subtitles support through YouTube

Embedded YouTube videos inside the website

Clean and responsive user interface

QR code support for offline exhibitions

Easy-to-navigate story archive
